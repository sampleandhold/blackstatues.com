  • Arlene's Grocery
    New York City
    June 5 2012

Formed in late 2010 as a songwriting duo by Maude and Ed, foisted upon the world as a 5-piece outfit comprised of some of the best players in NYC on June 5, 2012.

Debut album in the works for fall 2012, to be produced by Earl Slick. According to Frankie Teardrop over at systemsofromance.com:

"...caught their very first gig last week and it hit a sweet spot between Xmal, Siouxsie, Asmodi Bizarr, and Diamond Dogs."

Photography by D Horton

Maude Quill - Vocals
Edward Raison - Guitar
Steven Vallone - Keyboards
Quincy Lehr - Bass Guitar
Joe Izzo - Drums
